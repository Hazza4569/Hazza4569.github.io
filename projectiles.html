Projectile Motion

This is a demonstration of how projectiles follow a simple parabolic path when thrown. Overlayed on top the video upon clicking on analyse, is the cyan line that traces out the path taken by the projectile, red arrows to show horizontal and vertical velocity and a black arrow to show the resultant direction of the velocity.

Alongside the visual description overlayed on the video, three graphs are plotted when analyse is clicked. The graphs are of distance, velocity and acceleration all against time with the vertical and horizontal components plotted in different colours on the same graph.
